Agency Details

Football Agents Sierra Leone (FASL) has launched a nationwide initiative to ensure that all players, clubs, and coaches have proper representation. The organization aims to reach every corner where football is played, providing professional guidance to footballers and coaches for better career opportunities and contract negotiations. Led by President Osman Edward Kargbo, alongside Vice President Alhajie Maddi, Secretary General Ibrahim Koroma, Treasurer Mark Kargbo, and Communications Director Abdul Mohamed Mansaray, FASL is committed to building a structured and professional football system in Sierra Leone.

The organization believes that agents play a crucial role in protecting players' interests and securing fair deals in the football industry. Football Agency Sierra Leone is dedicated to scouting, developing, and representing top football talent. We focus on career management, player development, and connecting athletes with global opportunities. Our agency works with clubs, academies, and partners worldwide to help players reach their full potential.

Our Vision

Football Agents Sierra Leone (FASL) Expands to Support Players, Clubs, and Coaches

Our Mission

Our mission is to support footballers at every stage of their career through personalized development, professional representation, mentorship, and long-term planning. We aim to create opportunities and empower athletes to achieve sustainable success. With football rapidly growing in the country, FASL urges all stakeholders to embrace professional representation for a stronger and more competitive football environment. By ensuring proper management and career guidance, the organization hopes to elevate Sierra Leonean football to international standards.
